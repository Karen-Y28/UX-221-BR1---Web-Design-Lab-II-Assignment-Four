Just like many other kids, I grew up in a house with a Wii. Some of my fondest memories are of playing Wii Sports with my family. Due to the age gap between my sisters and me it was hard for us to connect, but video games became a space where that distance didn’t matter as much. Games like New Super Mario Bros. Wii and Kirby’s Epic Yarn brought us together.

One memory that really stands out is when my sister came back from Japan and surprised me with my own DS Lite. I remember feeling overwhelmingly excited, not just because I finally had a handheld console of my own. My love for video games truly began.
