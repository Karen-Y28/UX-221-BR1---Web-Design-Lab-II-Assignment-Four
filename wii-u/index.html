Wii U

Although the Wii U is a complete failure for Nintendo I still do have fond memories of the Wii U. After all it is what I played Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild on. To this day that game is still one of my favourites. It’s hard to recapture the beauty that was that game at the time. Many have tried and many have failed. It truly is a beautiful game.
